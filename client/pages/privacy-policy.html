Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect the personal information of Kwara State indigenes who register on this platform to participate in government initiatives.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, age, gender, local government, ward, community, BVN, NIN, bank name, and bank account number.
  • System-generated Information: A unique identification code linked to your local government and ward.
  • Usage Information: Data regarding how you interact with the website.

Purpose of Data Collection

Your information is collected to enable you to participate in government initiatives, verify your identity, generate a unique ID, and communicate important updates.

How We Use Your Information

Your information will only be used for the purposes of identification, data analysis, and fraud prevention. We will not share your data with third parties except with government authorities or as required by law.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We will only share your data with relevant government agencies or in response to valid legal requests.

Data Security

We implement strong security measures, including encryption and access control, to protect your data.

Data Retention

Your personal information will be stored as long as needed for the purposes mentioned, after which it will be securely deleted.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data. You can also object to its processing under certain conditions.

Cookies and Tracking

This platform does not use cookies, but anonymous usage statistics may be collected.

Updates to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally, and any significant changes will be communicated.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the office of the Deputy Chief of Staff, Kwara State.
