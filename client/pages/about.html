Empowering Kwara Citizens through Government Support

This platform provides easy access to government programs and services.

About the Initiative

The Kwara State Government is committed to improving the lives of its citizens by providing access to various government programs and services. Managed by the Deputy Chief of Staff, this initiative helps indigenes of Kwara State benefit from state and federal programs through data collection and effective resource allocation.

How It Works

Our platform simplifies the process for citizens to register and gain access to government benefits. By filling out the form with your personal details, you can generate a unique identification code tied to your local government and ward.

  • Fill the form with your name, phone number, BVN, NIN, and other details.
  • Generate a unique ID tied to your local government and ward.
  • Data is securely stored, accessible only by authorized personnel.

Key Benefits

  • Easy access to government programs and services.
  • Unique identification code to prevent multiple registrations.
  • Secure and confidential data handling.
  • Timely allocation of benefits based on verified information.
