Get Started by Filling Out the Form

To benefit from government programs designed to uplift Kwara indigenes, simply fill out the form with the following details:

  • ✓ Name, phone number, age, and gender
  • ✓ Local government, ward, and community
  • ✓ BVN, NIN, bank name, and account number

Your information will be securely stored and used to:

  • ✓ Ensure you are considered for targeted government benefits
  • ✓ Prevent multiple registrations with a unique ID linked to your NIN or BVN
  • ✓ Allow the government to efficiently deliver services and resources to your local community

By providing accurate details, you play a part in ensuring effective governance and equitable resource distribution across Kwara State.
